Description

1C:Accounting 8 for Kyrgyzstan is designed to automate accounting and tax accounting at enterprises of the Kyrgyz Republic, in accordance with the current legislation, as well as for the preparation of mandatory (regulated) reporting forms in organizations engaged in any type of commercial activity: wholesale and retail trade, provision of services, production, etc.
The software product is implemented on the technological platform

"1C:Enterprise 8". "Accounting for Kyrgyzstan" is released in two versions: 1C:Accounting 8 for Kyrgyzstan. The basic version is for a small enterprise, where one accountant works in the program and does not need to change the standard solution for the specifics of accounting of the enterprise; 1C:Accounting 8 for Kyrgyzstan. PROF - is recommended when several users work in the program, as well as when it is possible to change the standard configuration for the specifics of accounting of a particular enterprise. The Training version of the program is intended for studying and mastering the features of the configuration "Accounting for Kyrgyzstan".

"1C:Accounting 8 for Kyrgyzstan" allows you to keep accounting and tax accounting of economic activities of several organizations. At the same time, for each organization you can set up a separate information base, as well as in a common information base to reflect the accounting of several organizations. In addition, for each organization you can assign its own accounting policy (in the field of accounting and tax accounting). Functionality of the program "1C:Accounting 8 for Kyrgyzstan"
"1C:Accounting 8 for Kyrgyzstan" is a universal program for automation of accounting and tax accounting. The program is based on a flexible technological platform of the system "1C:Enterprise 8", the capabilities of which allow you to create and modify a variety of business applications. The delivery set of the software product includes an application solution - configuration "Accounting for Kyrgyzstan".

"1C:Accounting 8 for Kyrgyzstan" is designed for accounting and tax accounting, as well as for the preparation of mandatory (regulated) reporting forms in organizations engaged in any type of commercial activity: wholesale and retail trade, services, production, etc.

Accounting and tax accounting are implemented in accordance with the current legislation of the Kyrgyz Republic.

Keeping records of activities of several organizations With the help of "1C:Accounting 8 for Kyrgyzstan" you can keep accounting and tax accounting of economic activities of several organizations. Accounting for each organization can be kept in a separate information base. At the same time, "1C:Accounting 8 for Kyrgyzstan" provides the possibility of accounting and tax accounting of several organizations in a common information base (and individual entrepreneurs can act as separate organizations). This is convenient if the economic activities of these organizations are closely related to each other: it is possible to use common lists of goods, counterparties (business partners), employees, warehouses (storage locations), etc., and to generate mandatory reporting separately.

Accounting of inventories For the organization of accounting of inventories, the following methods of valuation of inventories at their disposal are supported:
at average cost; at the cost of first-in-time acquisition of inventories (FIFO method). To support the FIFO method of valuation, batch accounting is maintained in inventory accounts. Different costing methods may be used for different inventory accounts (according to IAS 2).

Warehouse accounting Warehouses may have quantitative or quantity-accounting. In the first case, the valuation of goods and materials does not depend on the warehouse from which they are received. Warehouse accounting can be disabled if there is no need for it.

In "1C:Accounting 8 for Kyrgyzstan" inventory data are recorded, which are automatically reconciled with the accounting data. On the basis of the inventory is reflected the identification of surpluses and write-off of shortages.

Accounting of trade operations Accounting of operations of receipt and sale of goods and services is automated. When selling goods, invoices for payment are issued, delivery notes and invoices are issued. All wholesale trade operations are accounted for in the context of contracts with buyers and suppliers. Reflection of returns of goods from the buyer and the supplier is automated.
